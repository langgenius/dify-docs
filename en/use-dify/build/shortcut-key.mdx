**[Go to Anything](/en/use-dify/build/goto-anything)**: Press `Cmd+K` (macOS) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows) anywhere in Dify to search and jump to everythingâ€”apps, plugins, knowledge bases, even workflow nodes. Use slash commands like `/theme` to change appearance, `/language` to switch languages, or `/help` to access documentation.
