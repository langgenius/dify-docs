A role is a named bundle of workspace-level permissions, such as installing plugins or managing model credentials. You manage roles in **Settings** > **Roles & Permissions**, then assign them to members so each person gets only the access they need.

Roles cover workspace-wide abilities like creating apps and knowledge bases. To control who can access each app and knowledge base, and what they can do with it, go to **Settings** > [**Resource Access**](/en/use-dify/workspace/access-rules).

## Role Types

- **System roles**

    A built-in set: Workspace Owner, Workspace Manager, Workspace Editor, Member, and Restricted Member. They can only be viewed or duplicated.

- **Custom roles**

    Additional roles that fit how your team works. A new workspace starts with any custom roles your system administrator has predefined in the admin console.

    Deleting a custom role detaches it from every member, resource access rule, and resource it was bound to.

## Roles and Members

You assign and change a member's roles from **Settings** > [**Members**](/en/use-dify/workspace/team-members-management).

When a member holds multiple roles, their permissions *combine*. For example, a member who holds both Editor and a custom role can do anything either one allows.

Adding a role only grants more permissions, never removes any. Anything a member's roles don't grant stays out of reach.
