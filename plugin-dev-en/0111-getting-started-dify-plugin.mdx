Launching a Dify plugin is the fastest way to tailor the platform to your product vision. In this guide you will learn how to compose modular capabilities that expand your AI experiences, integrate external systems, and ship production-ready automation.

<Frame caption="Marketplace">
  <img src="/images/marketplace.png" />
</Frame>

With plugins you can:
- Connect to external APIs across your stack
- Orchestrate data processing pipelines
- Deliver specialized computations
- Trigger actions inside and outside your application
